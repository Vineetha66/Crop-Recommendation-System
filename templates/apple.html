APPLE

Crop Overview

Apples are primarily produced in regions with temperate climates such as Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, and Uttarakhand in India.

Suitable temperatures for apple cultivation range from 10°C to 30°C.The typical growing season for apples in India extends from April to September.

Growth and Requirements

The duration from planting to harvest for apples ranges from about 3 to 5 years.

Apples require well-drained soil and regular irrigation.Fertilizers needed include nitrogen-rich fertilizers, phosphorus, potassium, zinc, and boron.

Cost and Yield

The average cost to produce apples is typically around INR 3 to 5 lakhs per acre..

Apples can yield around 10 to 15 tons per acre, depending on the variety and cultivation practices.
